What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They help the website recognize your device and remember certain information about your visit, such as your preferences and login status.

Similar technologies include:

  • Local Storage: Data stored in your browser that persists even after you close it
  • Session Storage: Data stored temporarily for a single browsing session
  • Web Beacons: Small image files used to track user activity
