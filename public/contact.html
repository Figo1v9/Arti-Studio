How Can We Help?

General Inquiries

Have questions about Arti Studio, our features, or how to use the platform? Send us an email and we'll be happy to help you get started.

Technical Support

Experiencing issues with your account, uploads, or any other technical problems? Please include:

  • A description of the issue you're experiencing
  • Your browser and device information
  • Screenshots if applicable
  • Your account email (if you have an account)

Content & Copyright Issues

If you believe content on our platform infringes your intellectual property rights, please visit our DMCA page for information on submitting a takedown request.

Feedback & Suggestions

We're always looking to improve Arti Studio. Your feedback helps us build a better platform for the entire community. Don't hesitate to share your ideas!

Business & Partnerships

Interested in collaborating with Arti Studio? We're open to partnerships, sponsorships, and business opportunities. Contact us to discuss how we can work together.

Frequently Asked Questions

Before contacting us, you might find your answer in our FAQ:

  • How do I create an account? Click "Create Account" and follow the registration process. You can also sign up using your Google account.
  • Is Arti Studio free? Yes! Browsing and basic features are completely free. Premium features are available for creators who want additional tools.
  • How do I upload my AI art? After logging in, click the upload button, add your image, include the prompt you used, and select a category.
  • Can I delete my content? Yes, you can delete your uploads at any time from your profile.
  • How do I report inappropriate content? Use the report button on any content, or email us directly.

Response Times

  • General inquiries: 24-48 hours
  • Technical support: 24-48 hours
  • DMCA requests: Within 72 hours (as required by law)
  • Business inquiries: 3-5 business days

Note: For urgent security issues or to report illegal content, please mark your email as "URGENT" in the subject line for priority handling.
